Typography check Your pages should use consistent font choices throughout — ideally one heading font and one body font. Body text should be 9–14pt maximum. Avoid Chalkboard. Good choices for fashion portfolios: Andale Mono, Orator Std, Typewriter Condensed, or a clean sans-serif. Font choices are part of your visual identity and are assessed.
Harvard referencing reminder Every image you did not make yourself must be credited on the page it appears. Use this format:
Artist, I. (Year) Title of work [medium]. Available at: URL (Accessed: DD Month YYYY).
Surname, I. (Year) Title of website page. Site name. Available at: URL (Accessed: DD Month YYYY).
