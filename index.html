Portfolio Checklist

Use this guide to ensure your portfolio meets all criteria

Portfolio Structure (25 pages)
Recommended Structure:
  1. Title Page - Name, ID, Project, Group
  2. Introduction - Project overview
  3. Research - ~10 pages
  4. Design Development - ~10 pages
  5. Similar but Different - 1 page
  6. Product Page - 1 page
  7. Final Line-Up - 1 page
1. Title Page

Include your name, student ID, project title, and IPF group

2. Introduction Page

Clear project context, inspiration, and what you'll explore

3. Research Pages (~10)

Diverse sources, quotes, visual research, inspiration

4. Design Development (~10)

2D/3D ideas, sketches, iterations, refinement process

5. Similar but Different Page

Show variety of similar design ideas with differences

6. Product Page

Hero shot of your final product/design

7. Final Line-Up

Complete collection/range shown together

Research Quality
Diverse Sources

Include various types of research and inspiration

  • Artist/designer case studies
  • Historical or cultural context
  • Material/technique research
  • Market/trend analysis
Personal Response

Show your own thinking and analysis of research

  • What you learned
  • How it influences your work
  • Critical evaluation
Visual Design
Typography

Consistent, readable font choices

  • Body text: 12-14pt max
  • ONE heading + ONE body font
  • Clear hierarchy
Colour Palette

Consistent colours linked to your theme

Layout & Flow

Clear visual hierarchy and page flow

Harvard Referencing
Proper Referencing

Credit all sources correctly

Format:
Last Name, Initial. (Year). Title [Image].
